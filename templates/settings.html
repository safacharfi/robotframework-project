Account Settings

Profile Settings

Update your profile information here, including your username, email address, and profile picture. Make sure your details are up to date for better account management.

You can also change your display name and customize your profile to reflect your identity within the app.

Security Settings

Your account security is important to us. We recommend setting a strong password, enabling two-factor authentication (2FA), and regularly changing your password for enhanced security.

If you suspect any unauthorized access to your account, please change your password immediately. To reset your password, follow the instructions on the reset page.

Notification Preferences

Here you can manage your notification preferences. You can choose to receive notifications via email or SMS for various events, such as new messages, updates, or reminders.

Choose your preferred notification methods and make sure you never miss important updates.

Appearance and Theme

Customize the look of your interface by selecting a light or dark theme. You can switch between the themes at any time to suit your preference.

A dark theme can be easier on the eyes, especially in low-light environments, while the light theme is suitable for a more traditional look and feel.
